Why now

Everything you trust gets inspected by certified eyes.

Nondestructive Testing is how the modern world stays trustworthy. The hardware is evolving fast — the people who can certify it are the bottleneck. That's your opening. Watch the particles — they know where they're needed.

Energy

The wind never stops. Neither does inspection.

Turbine blades, tower welds, foundation bolts — wind farms live outdoors in the worst weather on earth. NDT technicians keep them turning, on rope, by drone, and on the ground.

Aerospace

Every flight you've ever taken was signed off first.

Airframes, engine fans, landing gear — aircraft are inspected their whole working lives. New aerospace programs need first inspections; the existing fleet needs them forever.

Infrastructure

Bridges don't get to fail.

Aging steel, welds, and pins need re-certification — and there's a lot of aging steel out there. The work is here; the certified workforce isn't. Get certified while the door is wide open.

Roller coasters get crawled over by inspectors before you ever board. Welds, track, bolts, restraints — somebody's eyes cleared your last ride. They could be yours.
